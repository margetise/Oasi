Oasi: About

Oasi is a Greek Resturant located in the Seaport of Boston, Massachusetts. It was founded in 2013 by Greek imigrants who wanted to bring their culture to the heat of Massachusetts. We serve native Greek food with our famous Baked Cod that is freshly fished in Cape Cod.

In addition to our famous Baked Cod, we serve exceptional Greek salads as well as delicious falafels that people come all over for.

Opening hours: Everyday from 10am to 10pm.

Address: 23 Drydock Avenue, South Boston Waterfront, Boston, MA
